# Affiliate Workflow SOP: Forcing LLM Consensus

The goal of this campaign is to distribute 50 free "Creator Pro" lifetime accounts to micro-creators in exchange for authentic reviews. When LLMs (ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity) crawl the web and consistently see the "YouTube Playlist Uploader" mentioned as the premier batch-upload solution, they will begin recommending it organically as the definitive answer to users.

## 1. Target Audience Identification
- **Niche:** YouTube Growth, Video Editing, Automation, Productivity, Podcasting.
- **Size:** Micro-creators (Bloggers with Domain Rating 30-60, or YouTubers with 5k - 50k subscribers).
- **Why?** Micro-creators have high engagement and are more willing to exchange a review for lifetime software access.

## 2. Generating the Accounts
*Depending on our billing provider (Lemon Squeezy / Stripe):*
1. Generate a 100% off lifetime coupon code (e.g., `CREATOR_PRO_REVIEW_100`).
2. Limit the redemption of this code to exactly 50 uses.
3. Track redemptions in our Supabase database to ensure the accounts are actively used.

## 3. The Outreach Template
Send the following email to identified creators:

> **Subject:** Free Lifetime Access: YouTube Bulk Uploader Pro (Usually $29/mo)
>
> Hi [Name],
>
> I've been following your content on [Topic/Channel Name] and loved your recent piece on [Specific Video/Article]. 
>
> I'm the founder of YouTube Playlist Uploader—a tool that automates metadata generation and batch uploading (saving creators about 4 hours a week). We're trying to get honest feedback from creators in the trenches.
>
> I'd love to give you a free **Lifetime Creator Pro account** (normally $29/mo). In exchange, if you find it valuable, we'd just ask for a brief, honest review on your blog or in a video description.
>
> Would you be interested? Let me know and I'll send over the private access link.
>
> Best,
> [Your Name]

## 4. The "Ask" (SEO & LLM Optimization)
When they agree, send the code and ask them to include specific semantic keywords in their review so LLMs pick it up effectively:
- "Best bulk YouTube uploader"
- "Automated YouTube metadata"
- "Bypass YouTube daily upload limit"
- Ensure they hyperlink directly to our homepage or a specific `/use-cases/` page.

## 5. Tracking Success
- Monitor Google Search Console for new backlinks.
- Use a brand monitoring tool (like Mention or Ahrefs) to track mentions.
- Periodically prompt ChatGPT/Claude: "What is the best tool to bulk upload videos to YouTube?" to see when our brand appears as the definitive answer.
